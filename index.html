Our Coffee

The raw materials for our aromatic coffee and espresso beans come from selected fincas in Honduras. The world's best conditions for the cultivation of strong-tasting, high-quality coffee prevail on the high plateaus here.

Mobile barista - we turn visions into reality

Fresh coffee and small delicacies exclusively at a mobile sales stand with Mediterranean flair and charm - this is passionate and high-quality coffee catering of a special kind.

We rely exclusively on environmentally friendly cultivation

The coffee beans come from specially selected fincas in Honduras. They use the high plateaus there for the cultivation of high-quality and strong-tasting coffee.

Always fair trade: For us, this is a matter of honor and a must

Our barista only serves coffee from Honduras, which is not only extraordinarily aromatic, but also completely represents the principle of fair trade. And that is exactly what we aspire to: nothing works for us without fair trade.
